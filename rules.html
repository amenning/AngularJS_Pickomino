Goal: Collect the most worms!

Step 1. Roll the dice.

  • If this is your first roll this turn, go to Step 2.
  • If the result does not include anything you have not already collected, your turn ends immediately. You must take the top tile from your stack (if any) and place it back on the grill. If you return a worm, then flip over the highest valued tile on the grill, unless it is the tile just returned. The flipped over tile is out of the game.

Step 2. Select one number/symbol from the resulting dice roll and collect all matching dice, adding them to your collection.

  • If a worm is not among your collected dice, go to Step 1.
  • If the sum of your collected dice (worms are worth five) is less than the value of every face-up tile on the grill, and is not equal to any of the tiles on top of other players' stacks, go to Step 1.

  • Otherwise make a choice:
    • Go to Step 1

      • OR

    • Take any worm of equal or less value than your frozen dice sum from the grill
